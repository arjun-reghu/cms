Project Report
Centralized CMS & Asset Management System (AMS)
1. Introduction

The Centralized CMS & Asset Management System is a web-based enterprise platform designed to manage:

Branch infrastructure

Employee records

IT assets

Non-IT assets

Onboarding & offboarding assistance tickets

Agreements and renewals

System users

Audit logs and notifications

The application provides role-based access control, allowing different departments to interact with the system based on their responsibilities.

The system is designed for organizations with multiple branches and hundreds of employees, ensuring centralized control and traceability.

2. System Objectives

The system aims to:

Maintain branch infrastructure information

Manage IT and Non-IT assets

Track asset assignment to employees

Manage employee onboarding and offboarding

Track agreements and renewals

Maintain audit trails

Generate reports and exports

Provide role-based dashboards

13. Ticket Workflow
Onboarding

HR creates ticket.

Technician processes ticket:

Actions:

create email

assign laptop

assign bag

assign accessories

Ticket closes when all required resources are assigned.

Offboarding

HR submits employee codes.

Technician must:

disable email

disable AD account

collect laptop

collect bag

collect accessories

Ticket closes when all resources are returned.

25. Asset Lifecycle Flow

Every asset moves through four lifecycle stages.

Asset Purchased
       │
       ▼
Stock Entry
       │
       ▼
Assigned to Branch or Employee
       │
       ▼
Returned to Stock
       │
       ▼
Scrapped / Disposed
Detailed Lifecycle
1 Stock
New assets added to inventory
Status = STOCK
2 Assigned
Assigned to employee or branch
Status = ASSIGNED

Tracked in:

asset_history
3 Returned
Returned during employee offboarding
Status = STOCK
4 Scrap
Old / damaged assets removed
Status = SCRAP

Optional additional field:

scrap_reason
scrap_date
