Completion Report
Centralized CMS & Asset Management System (AMS)
Date: 09 March 2026

1. Project Overview

The Centralized CMS & Asset Management System has been successfully developed as a web-based enterprise platform for managing:

Branch infrastructure
Employee records
IT and Non-IT assets
Onboarding & offboarding assistance tickets
Agreements and renewals
System users with role-based access
Audit logs and notifications

The application implements role-based access control with five distinct roles, each with specific privileges and dedicated dashboards.
